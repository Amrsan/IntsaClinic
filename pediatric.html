Pediatric Services

Compassionate, expert care for infants, children, and adolescents. Our pediatricians at Insta Clinic provide preventive care, diagnosis, and treatment for a wide range of childhood conditions, ensuring your child's health and development at every stage.

Our Pediatric Services

Insta Clinic offers comprehensive pediatric care, from routine checkups and immunizations to the management of acute and chronic illnesses. Our team is dedicated to supporting your child's physical, emotional, and developmental needs.

Well-Child Visits & Immunizations

Regular health checkups, growth monitoring, and vaccinations to keep your child healthy and protected from preventable diseases.

  • Comprehensive physical exams
  • Growth and development tracking
  • Routine and catch-up immunizations
  • Vision and hearing screening
  • Parental guidance and anticipatory advice

Acute Illness & Injury Care

Prompt evaluation and treatment for fevers, infections, respiratory illnesses, minor injuries, and other urgent pediatric concerns.

  • Fever and infection management
  • Respiratory and ear infections
  • Minor injury and wound care
  • Allergy and asthma attacks
  • Same-day sick visits

Chronic Conditions & Developmental Care

Ongoing management of asthma, allergies, diabetes, and developmental or behavioral concerns, with a focus on family-centered care.

  • Asthma and allergy management
  • Diabetes care and education
  • Developmental and behavioral assessments
  • Care coordination with specialists
  • Support for chronic and complex conditions

Child-Friendly Technology & Facilities

Modern, Safe, and Comfortable Environment

Our clinic is equipped with advanced pediatric medical technology and a welcoming environment designed to make children and families feel at ease. We use gentle, age-appropriate approaches for all procedures and diagnostics.

  • Digital growth and development tracking
  • Child-friendly vaccination and testing rooms
  • Non-invasive diagnostic tools
  • Comfortable play and waiting areas
  • Family education resources
