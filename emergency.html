Emergency Medical Services

Immediate care when you need it most. Our emergency medical team is available 24/7 to provide rapid response and life-saving treatments for critical situations.

Our Emergency Services

At Insta Clinic, we provide comprehensive emergency medical services with state-of-the-art equipment and highly trained professionals ready to respond to any medical crisis.

Trauma Care

Immediate treatment for serious injuries including fractures, wounds, burns, and other traumatic injuries requiring urgent medical attention.

Defibrillator emergency services

We can provide defibrillator emergency services for cardiac emergencies, including heart attacks, heart failure, and arrhythmias requiring immediate intervention.

Respiratory Emergencies

Treatment for severe asthma attacks, respiratory distress, and other breathing emergencies with oxygen therapy and respiratory support.

OverDose Emergencies

We provide immediate care for overdose emergencies, including drug overdose, alcohol poisoning, and other life-threatening situations.
