Orthopedic Services

Comprehensive orthopedic care for bones, joints, muscles, and ligaments. Our expert orthopedic team at Insta Clinic provides advanced diagnostics, treatment, and rehabilitation for injuries and chronic conditions, helping you regain mobility and live pain-free.

Our Orthopedic Services

Insta Clinic offers a full spectrum of orthopedic services, from sports injuries and trauma care to joint replacement and pediatric orthopedics. We use the latest techniques and technology to ensure the best outcomes for every patient.

Fracture & Trauma Care

Immediate assessment and management of fractures, sprains, and musculoskeletal injuries, including casting, splinting, and surgical intervention when needed.

  • On-site fracture assessment and stabilization
  • Splinting and casting for broken bones
  • Management of sprains and soft tissue injuries
  • Surgical repair for complex fractures
  • Follow-up care and rehabilitation

Pediatric Orthopedics

Specialized care for children with bone, joint, or growth disorders, including congenital deformities, scoliosis, and sports injuries.

  • Treatment of congenital bone deformities
  • Management of scoliosis and spinal disorders
  • Growth plate injury care
  • Pediatric fracture management
  • Sports injury prevention and treatment

Common Orthopedic Conditions We Treat

Our orthopedic specialists diagnose and manage a wide range of musculoskeletal conditions for all ages.

Bone Fractures & Dislocations

Expert care for simple and complex fractures, dislocations, and trauma-related injuries.

Sports Injuries

Treatment for ligament tears, tendon injuries, sprains, strains, and overuse injuries.

Pediatric Orthopedic Disorders

Management of congenital deformities, growth plate injuries, and scoliosis in children.

Joint Pain & Arthritis

Diagnosis and treatment of osteoarthritis, rheumatoid arthritis, and chronic joint pain.
