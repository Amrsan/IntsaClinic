General Surgery Services

Comprehensive surgical care with a focus on safety, precision, and patient well-being. Our expert general surgeons at Insta Clinic provide advanced surgical solutions for a wide range of conditions, ensuring the highest standards of care from consultation to recovery.

Our General Surgery Services

Insta Clinic offers a full spectrum of general surgical procedures, from minimally invasive techniques to complex operations. Our multidisciplinary team utilizes the latest technology and evidence-based practices to deliver optimal outcomes for every patient.

Laparoscopic (Keyhole) Surgery

Minimally invasive procedures for gallbladder removal, appendectomy, hernia repair, and more. Benefits include smaller incisions, less pain, and faster recovery.

  • Gallbladder removal (cholecystectomy)
  • Appendectomy
  • Hernia repair (inguinal, umbilical, incisional)
  • Exploratory laparoscopy
  • Shorter hospital stay and recovery time

Hernia Surgery

Expert repair of inguinal, umbilical, and incisional hernias using both open and laparoscopic techniques for durable, long-term results.

  • Inguinal hernia repair
  • Umbilical hernia repair
  • Incisional and ventral hernia repair
  • Minimally invasive and open approaches
  • Mesh and non-mesh options

Breast & Thyroid Surgery

Surgical management of benign and malignant breast and thyroid conditions, including biopsies, lumpectomies, mastectomies, and thyroidectomies.

  • Breast lump evaluation and biopsy
  • Lumpectomy and mastectomy
  • Thyroid nodule assessment and biopsy
  • Partial and total thyroidectomy
  • Parathyroid surgery

Specialized Surgical Procedures

Our surgeons are skilled in a variety of advanced procedures, ensuring safe and effective treatment tailored to your needs.

Appendectomy

Emergency and elective removal of the appendix, performed with minimally invasive techniques for rapid recovery.

Gallbladder Surgery (Cholecystectomy)

Laparoscopic and open removal of the gallbladder for gallstones, infections, and other biliary conditions.

Hemorrhoid & Anal Surgery

Surgical and non-surgical management of hemorrhoids, anal fissures, fistulas, and pilonidal disease.

Skin & Soft Tissue Surgery

Excision of cysts, lipomas, abscess drainage, and treatment of skin lesions with a focus on minimal scarring.
