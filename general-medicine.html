General Medicine Services

Comprehensive primary and preventive care for adults and adolescents. Our experienced general medicine physicians at Insta Clinic provide expert diagnosis, management, and follow-up for a wide range of acute and chronic medical conditions, focusing on your long-term health and well-being.

Our General Medicine Services

Insta Clinic offers a full spectrum of general medical services, from preventive health checks to the management of complex chronic diseases. Our team uses evidence-based medicine and a patient-centered approach to ensure the best outcomes for every individual.

Preventive Health & Checkups

Routine physical exams, health screenings, vaccinations, and lifestyle counseling to help you stay healthy and detect issues early.

  • Annual physical examinations
  • Blood pressure and cholesterol screening
  • Vaccinations and immunizations
  • Cancer screening (breast, colon, prostate, etc.)
  • Lifestyle and nutrition counseling

Chronic Disease Management

Ongoing care for diabetes, hypertension, asthma, thyroid disorders, and other chronic conditions, with personalized treatment plans and regular monitoring.

  • Diabetes and blood sugar management
  • Hypertension and heart disease care
  • Asthma and COPD management
  • Thyroid and hormonal disorder follow-up
  • Medication adjustment and monitoring

Acute Illness & Symptom Management

Rapid assessment and treatment for infections, fevers, respiratory illnesses, digestive complaints, and other acute medical issues.

  • Fever and infection evaluation
  • Respiratory and flu-like illness care
  • Digestive and urinary symptoms
  • Minor injuries and wound care
  • Short-term medication prescriptions

Our Approach to General Medicine

We believe in building long-term relationships with our patients, focusing on prevention, education, and personalized care at every stage of life.

Patient-Centered Medical Care

Our physicians take the time to listen, educate, and involve you in every decision about your health.

  • Thorough medical history and physical examination
  • Preventive screenings and risk assessment
  • Evidence-based diagnosis and treatment
  • Coordination with specialists when needed
