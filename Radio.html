Non-invasive imaging for abdominal, pelvic, thyroid, vascular, and pregnancy assessments. Safe for all ages, including prenatal and pediatric care in your home for your safety and comfort.

  • Abdominal and pelvic ultrasound
  • Pregnancy and fetal assessment
  • Thyroid and neck imaging
  • Vascular Doppler studies
  • Pediatric and adult applications
